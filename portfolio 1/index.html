Tomato Basil Buratta Pasta

Linda Haas
  • Preparation Time
  • Cooking Time
  • Serves
  • :10 min.
  • :15 min.
  • :4

A succulent but healthy meal that brings on Mexican heat with the lightness of a flour tortilla. Easy to prepare for quick meals or mass produce it when company knocks at your door.

Ingredients

  • 680 g. plum tomatoes, quartered
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 125 ml. olive oil
  • 12 Tbs. red wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp. olive oil
  • 340 g. spagehetti
  • 10 g. chopped basil
  • 30 g. grated parmesan cheese
  • 375 g. burrata cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large, nonreactive bowl, combine the tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, vinegar, 1/2 tsp. salt and 1/4 tsp. pepper. Let stand at room temperature, sirring occasionally, for 1 hour.
  2. In a large, nonreactive bowl, combine the tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, vinegar, 1/2 tsp. salt and 1/4 tsp. pepper. Let stand at room temperature, sirring occasionally, for 1 hour.

  4. In a large, nonreactive bowl, combine the tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, vinegar, 1/2 tsp. salt and 1/4 tsp. pepper. Let stand at room temperature, sirring occasionally, for 1 hour.
